JEFFREY BOX - HEALTHCARE DATA SCIENTIST

"In God we trust. All others must bring data." ~ W. Edwards Deming

We live in a world overrun by data. Datageddon. Every day, we create as much data as we did since the beginning of the world until 2003 (E. Schmidt). Presently, 2.5 quintillion bytes of data are created each day (Forbes). Most industries are leveraging this information for value; however, Healthcare is lagging. This is the industry responsible for not only our well-being but ~20% of our GDP. It is my job to use Statistics, Data Science, and Business Intelligence to help Healthcare organizations improve quality, safety, processes, and decision-making. My portfolio contains information about me, links to connect, and sample Data Science projects. Enjoy!
